Three Converging Crises

America faces simultaneous challenges of unprecedented scale

CRISIS #1: POLITICAL FRACTURE

Social Division Approaching Civil War Levels

  • Americans live in parallel realities with incompatible facts
  • Political polarization hardened into mutual dehumanization (Pew Research 2024)
  • Violence simmers beneath political discourse
  • Democratic norms crumbling under competing national visions
  • Return of Gilded Age plutocracy with extreme wealth concentration (Oxfam 2024)

Scale: 1850s-level division that preceded Civil War

CRISIS #2: ECONOMIC DISRUPTION

AI Displacement Rivaling the Great Depression

  • 40% of employers expect AI-driven workforce reduction (WEF 2025)
  • Entire professions face automation: law, medicine, accounting, journalism
  • Decades of change compressed into years
  • Middle-class economic security disappearing faster than opportunities emerge
  • 85 million jobs projected for displacement by 2025 (WEF 2020)

Scale: 1929-level economic devastation with technological acceleration

CRISIS #3: CLIMATE EMERGENCY

Environmental Collapse Threatening Survival

  • Environmental collapse threatens human survival
  • Climate disasters overwhelm weakened institutions (IPCC 2023)
  • Divided societies cannot coordinate response at required scale
  • Future generations inherit uninhabitable planet
  • Collective action impossible without functional democracy

Scale: Existential threat requiring mobilization beyond WWII

Why These Crises Amplify Each Other

These are not separate problems requiring separate solutions—they feed on each other, accelerating democratic breakdown

Political + Economic = Plutocracy

  • Economic desperation fuels political extremism
  • Corporate capture corrupts democratic institutions
  • Polarization prevents economic policy coordination
  • Wealth concentration makes inequality permanent

Political + Climate = Paralysis

  • Social fracture prevents collective climate action
  • Climate disasters increase social stress and conflict
  • Authoritarian governments prioritize short-term control
  • Climate migration strains weakened institutions

Economic + Climate = Collapse

  • Climate costs overwhelm disrupted economies
  • Economic insecurity prevents sustainable choices
  • Fossil fuel interests block economic transition
  • Climate adaptation requires massive economic coordination

All Three Together = Death Spiral

  • Trust collapse prevents institutional solutions
  • Economic insecurity eliminates civic participation
  • Climate chaos overwhelms social systems
  • Democratic governance becomes impossible
